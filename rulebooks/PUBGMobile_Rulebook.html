⚡ PUBG Mobile Tournament Rulebook ⚡

CSE DAY 2K26 - Esports Hub

Entry Fee: 200 BDT/Team | Platform: Mobile | Team Size: 4 Players

📖 Tournament Rules & Settings

  • Mode: Classic TPP (Third Person Perspective)
  • Map: Erangel
  • Team Size: Squad (4 players per team)
  • Match Duration: Until last team standing
  • Settings: Default tournament settings
  • Device: Participants must bring their own Android device
  • Communication: In-game voice chat allowed
  • Fair Play: No cheating, hacking, or unauthorized modifications
  • Disqualification: Any form of misconduct leads to team disqualification

🏆 Prize Structure

  • 1st Place: Trophy + Certificates + Prize Money
  • 2nd Place: Certificates + Prize Money
  • 3rd Place: Certificates + Prize Money
  • All Teams: Certificates & Snacks

⚠️ Important Notes

  • All team members must arrive 30 minutes before match time
  • Bring valid student ID for verification
  • Ensure PUBG Mobile is installed and updated
  • Stable internet connection required
  • Judges' decision is final and binding
  • Any technical issues must be reported immediately
  • Teams must register with complete player details

🎮 Match Format

  • Round 1: Qualification round (top teams advance)
  • Round 2: Semi-finals
  • Final: Championship match
  • Scoring: Based on placement and kills
